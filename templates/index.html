01

Text Preprocessing

Input is lowercased, URLs stripped, and special characters removed. Headline and article are concatenated for joint analysis.

02

TF-IDF Vectorization

Text is converted to a numerical vector using Term Frequency–Inverse Document Frequency with 1-2 word n-grams (10,000 features). This captures which words are most distinctive for fake vs real news.

03

Logistic Regression Classification

A Logistic Regression classifier (trained on labeled fake/real data) predicts the probability of the article being fake or real. Output is a calibrated probability score.

04

Signal Extraction

Parallel rule-based analysis extracts 4 explainability signals: Emotional Manipulation, Factual Specificity, Source Credibility, and Logical Coherence.

05

Verdict & Explanation

Signals + ML probability are combined into a final verdict (FAKE / REAL / UNCERTAIN) with red flags, green flags, and a human-readable explanation.
